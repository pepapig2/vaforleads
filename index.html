Process

How It Works

The same six steps we run on every project — refined over 275+ engagements.

  1. 1

    Understand Your Ideal Customer

    We start with a discovery call to map out your ICP, your offer, and what you're trying to achieve with outbound.

  2. 2

    Build Target Prospect Criteria

    We turn that into specific filters — company size, industry, title, location — so every search stays on target.

  3. 3

    Research Companies

    We identify and qualify companies that actually fit your criteria, one by one — not by pulling a generic list.

  4. 4

    Find Decision Makers

    Sales Navigator and manual digging pinpoint the real buyers and influencers — not just whoever has a public email.

  5. 5

    Verify Contact Details

    Every email and phone number gets checked through Hunter and NeverBounce before it goes anywhere near your CRM.

  6. 6

    Deliver Clean Spreadsheet

    You get an organized spreadsheet, formatted exactly how your team needs it and ready to import.

FAQ

Questions Teams Ask Before Hiring Us

Straight answers to what comes up most on discovery calls.

How accurate is the data you deliver?

Every contact is manually researched, then passed through Hunter and NeverBounce for email verification before it's delivered. Our recent case studies average 94–97% deliverability.

How long does a typical project take?

Most lists of 500–1,500 contacts are delivered within 5–10 business days, depending on scope and criteria complexity. You'll get an exact timeline on your discovery call.

Is my target list and data kept confidential?

Yes. Your ideal customer profile, search criteria, and delivered data are never shared, reused for other clients, or resold. Your outbound strategy stays yours.

What industries do you work with?

We've delivered projects across SaaS, recruitment, marketing agencies, construction, healthcare, and professional services, tailoring our research methodology to each.

What does it cost to get started?

Pricing depends on list size, criteria complexity, and turnaround. Book a free discovery call and we'll send a clear, itemized quote within 24 hours — no pressure, no obligation.

Do you offer any guarantees?

If a delivered contact bounces due to a verification error on our end, we'll research and replace it at no additional cost.
